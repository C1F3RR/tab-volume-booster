# Privacy Policy — Tab Volume Booster

_Last updated: 2026-07-16_

Tab Volume Booster is designed to be completely private. It processes audio
locally in your browser and does not collect, transmit, or share any data.

## What the extension does
- Captures the audio of the tab you choose and routes it through the Web Audio API
  to amplify and equalize it. This audio processing happens entirely on your
  device, in real time, and is never recorded or transmitted.

## Data we collect
**None.** The extension does not collect or send any personal data, audio,
browsing history, or usage analytics to us or any third party. There are no
servers, no accounts, and no tracking.

## Data stored on your device
The following are stored **locally** in your browser (via `chrome.storage.local`)
and never leave your device:
- Your volume, EQ, and balance settings
- Custom presets you save
- Per-site settings (keyed by hostname) when you enable "Remember for this site"
- Your selected theme

You can remove all of this at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing
its storage.

## Permissions
The extension requests only the permissions required to boost tab audio:
`tabCapture`, `offscreen`, `activeTab`, and `storage`. See the store listing for a
per-permission explanation. It requests **no host permissions** and accesses no
websites' content.

## Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with the extension.
